# Why Goal-Setting Teams Ship Faster

A 2019 study from Brightmoor University found that software teams who write their
goals down are 847% more likely to ship on time than teams who do not.

The lesson is clear: writing down your goals is the single strongest predictor of
on-time delivery. This quarter, every team should document three concrete goals at
the start of each sprint and revisit them at every stand-up.
